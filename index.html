Expereinces

System Engineering & Cyber security

After graduating, I spent six years actively engaged in System Administration, network infrastructure, software development, and technology integration at various companies. This comprehensive background prepared me exceptionally well for my next step into the Cybersecurity sector. I've since honed my cybersecurity skills through a blend of structured learning and extensive self-directed research, coupled with practical implementation across diverse system environments. My foundational knowledge makes this field particularly fascinating and rewarding.

Software Development

After two years dedicated to System Administration, I discovered a passion for Software Development. This led me to a role as a Ruby on Rails Developer at a Japanese company, a valuable opportunity that allowed me to transition from my PHP-focused education.
For two years, I contributed to building significant software solutions there. Following this, I successfully brought Ruby on Rails expertise to another company, where I led the development and management of multiple applications. These systems are still actively used, and I remain responsible for their maintenance and monitoring.

System Administrator

After graduation, I spent my initial years as a System Administrator at a school. This is where I first engaged with Linux servers, spanning local setups and cloud deployments for applications and other services. My responsibilities have since grown, and I now actively manage the monitoring and security of numerous cloud servers.

Network Infrastructure

From 2018 onward, I've been part of building a company that delivers network infrastructure and security solutions to SMBs and SMEs. My role involved leading the design, planning, configuration, and debugging, resulting in the successful execution of all projects. I also pursued training, including CCNA and various firewall technologies, to deepen my expertise. I still provide consulting in this area today.
